Trips

To schedule a trip with us, contact us today!

Verdant Hills

Difficulty: ⭐

Admission: $29.99 per person

Age Requirement: 14+

A nice, relaxing rafting ride for rafters of all levels! Absorb the gorgeous greenery as you learn the basics of rafting.

Ozborne's Mouth

Difficulty: ⭐⭐

Admission: $29.99 per person

Age Requirement: 14+

All aboooaaard! Ozborne's Mouth is a course that's a step above beginner. With its titular rapids in the middle stretch, you'll be going off the rails!

Jagged Teeth River

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐

Admission: $39.99 per person

Age Requirement: 16+

Our most popular location, Jagged Teeth River provides narrow necks and a few rapids, while being accessible to casual rafters!

Dante's Chute

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Admission: $49.99 per person

Age Requirement: 18+

Live through this watery Inferno as you go through seven steep rapids in this fast-paced ride! You may even find your love on the other side...

X-treme Rapids

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Admission: $49.99 per person

Age Requirement: 18+

This trip isn't called X-treme without a reason... this river is rapids all the way down, with sharp turns and white water all around. This trip is for experienced rafters only, so hold onto your butts!
